In A Silent Room

Medium

Physical artwork created using acrylic and oil on canvas.

Context

This piece focuses on the quiet elegance of a piano and sheet music, captured in a romantic impressionistic style reminiscent of Bridgerton. The soft brushstrokes and warm tones create a sense of intimacy, drawing attention to the elegance of the objects themselves rather than any surrounding environment.
The scene feels like a moment frozen in time, where the piano and sheet music stand still, awaiting the music that could come to life at any moment. It's about the stillness and anticipation, the beauty in the simplicity of these elements, and the way they seem to hold a story all on their own.
