Reveries

Medium

Digital artwork created using Procreate.

Context

With "Reveries," I wanted to dive into the intricate world of fabrics and textures—velvet, lace, glitter, and more. This series is an exploration of how materials can tell their own story, even without the presence of a figure.

Each piece focuses entirely on the clothing, allowing the textures and folds to take center stage. I challenged myself to lean into a more realistic style, bringing out the richness and detail of every stitch, sparkle, and drape. It’s less about who might wear these garments and more about the quiet beauty of the materials themselves.

What to Look For

  • The way glitter catches the light—like it’s alive and constantly shifting.

  • Velvet folds that almost feel touchable, with shadows that give them weight and depth.

  • Lace patterns so detailed they’ll make you want to zoom in to see every little thread.

What's Next?

"Reveries" opened the door to even more possibilities for exploring textures. In future work, I want to push further—perhaps combining textures with movement or exploring fabrics in surreal settings.
